# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Business Context

Afromeet supports remote collaboration for teams and communities across Africa, where network conditions and device capabilities can be inconsistent. The platform aims to reduce friction in joining meetings while maintaining core collaboration features.

## Business Objectives

- Increase adoption by offering a fast and lightweight meeting experience.
- Reduce meeting drop-offs caused by complex onboarding or device issues.
- Build a trusted brand for remote collaboration in emerging markets.

## Stakeholders

- Product leadership
- Engineering and design
- Community managers and facilitators
- End users (hosts and participants)

## Scope

### In Scope

- Browser-based meeting creation and joining.
- Live meetings with audio/video and chat.
- Basic participant management (self mute/camera controls).

### Out of Scope

- Enterprise administration and SSO.
- Recording, transcription, or advanced analytics.
- Large webinar or broadcast capabilities.

## Business Requirements

- **BR-1:** Meetings must be created and shared in under one minute.
- **BR-2:** Participants must join without installing native apps.
- **BR-3:** The platform must function on mid-range devices and common browsers.
- **BR-4:** The experience must remain usable under degraded network conditions.
- **BR-5:** The platform must provide a clear, trustworthy pre-join experience.

## Constraints

- Limited backend infrastructure in early phases.
- Bandwidth variability and high latency in target regions.
- Budget constraints for real-time media infrastructure.

## Risks

- Media instability could reduce trust and adoption.
- High support needs if onboarding is unclear.
- Scaling costs may outpace revenue growth.

## Success Metrics

- Growth in monthly active hosts and participants.
- Meeting completion rate without drop-offs.
- Median time to join a meeting.
- Qualitative user satisfaction ratings.
